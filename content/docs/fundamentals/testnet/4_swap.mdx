---
title: Swap
---

After receiving the BIT10.BTC token, navigate to [bit10.app/swap](https://www.bit10.app/swap). Here you can swap your BIT10.BTC token to receive BIT10.DEFI token(s).

### How to swap

1. Select the number of BIT10.DEFI tokens you want to receive.

<p align='center'>
<img src='https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ZeyaRabani/Gitbook2/main/public/assets/swap_1.png' alt='Install' className='rounded' align='center' />
</p>

2. Click on the Trade button to buy BIT10.DEFI using your BIT10.BTC.
3. Approve the connection request for the ICP token canister.

<p align='center'>
<img src='https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ZeyaRabani/Gitbook2/main/public/assets/swap2.png' alt='Install' className='rounded' align='center' />
</p>

4. After approving the connection, approve the transaction.

<p align='center'>
<img src='https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ZeyaRabani/Gitbook2/main/public/assets/swap3.png' alt='Install' className='rounded' align='center' />
</p>

5. Once the transaction is successful, navigate to the **Portfolio** page to see the result of your transaction under the **Your Recent Activity** section.
